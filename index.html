About PetTrackQR

For many pet owners, the fear of losing a pet is heartbreaking. Sadly, thousands of lost pets remain unclaimed every year because finders can’t reach their families. PetTrackQR bridges this gap with scannable QR codes attached to pet collars, giving anyone who finds a lost pet immediate access to the owner's contact details and a way to bring them safely home.

How We Work

Register Your Pet

Pet owners can conveniently register their pets online by providing a few basic details about their pets along with the owner's contact information through a simple form.

QR Code Generation

Upon successful registration, a unique QR code is generated for each pet, which can be securely downloaded by the owner.

QR Code Attachment

Pet owners can conveniently attach the downloaded QR code to their pet’s collar, ensuring vital identification details are easily accessible when needed.

Lost Pet Reporting

When the QR code is scanned, the pet’s registered information is displayed, allowing the finder to promptly notify the owner regarding the lost pet.
